Product Analysis of Yura&co Fashion Periode January-October 2021

Business Background

Yura&co is Fashion Online Store that newly established in 2020, founded in Australia. Our sales focused on our official website and official mobile application.

In the December 2021, our Head of Marketing, feels our business is not going well in 2021 and found out we have some customers didn't generate revenue in 2021.

They want to see product performance in 10 months to understanding which product sell well and which product need to boost.

Problem Statements

  • How to increase revenue by 20% revenue in 1 year?

Insight

  • Customer actively purchase on Autumn March and Winter July. Also sometimes in Summer January, but rarely purchase on Spring.
  • Customers loves to shopping on Sunday.
  • Even though Denim revenue decreased on February, April, June, and keep decreased starting on August, Denim still become high demand.
